## Decision Required

Before KAT can write any customer-facing content for **stlouiscreations.com**, we need to lock the voice. Two candidates from the Shopify audit:

### Option 1 — Garage Workshop Warmth (current Shopify tone)
Informal, personal, craft-forward. Reads like the founder talking. "We" and "our shop." Occasional humor. Friendly, accessible, slightly DIY.

**When this works:** blog articles, About page, social posts, craft-explainer content.
**Pros:** differentiates from big-box competitors, builds personal trust.
**Cons:** can undermine premium positioning for corporate/awards orders.

### Option 2 — Corporate Precision (current Vercel tone)
Clean, professional, third-person. Feature/benefit structure. "St. Louis Creations" or "the studio." Understated.

**When this works:** corporate orders, services page, pricing, policy pages.
**Pros:** credibility for B2B, awards, fundraiser buyers.
**Cons:** feels cold for gift buyers and can read generic.

---

## Proposed Hybrid (pending approval)

**Core voice: Warm but professional.** Lean toward Option 1's personal warmth for product, blog, and About content. Lean toward Option 2's precision for policies, services, corporate, and pricing. Consistent across all surfaces:

- **First person plural** ("we") — not brand name in body copy
- **Active voice**, short sentences
- **Specific over generic** — "12oz double-wall stainless tumbler" not "premium drinkware"
- **Never jargony** — no "solutions," "leverage," "synergies"
- **Confident without hype** — no "revolutionary," "game-changing"
- **Warmth through specificity** — share the shop, the process, the materials — not buzzwords

---

## Vocabulary

### Preferred
- "laser engraving" (not "laser marking")
- "our shop" (informal contexts), "our studio" (corporate contexts)
- "custom" (not "bespoke" unless a luxury product context)
- "crafted" (sparingly — avoid it becoming a crutch)
- "Eureka, Missouri" (never just "Eureka" without state on first mention)

### Avoid
- "Precision-engineered"
- "Artisanal" (overused)
- "Premium" unless paired with specifics
- "Quality" as a standalone claim
